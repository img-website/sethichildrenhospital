Dr. Dileep Sethi – Leading with Compassion & Expertise

Director, Senior Consultant Pediatrics & Neonatology

Our director had completed his post graduation in 1983 from SMS Medical College. After passing out he served for 6 years in Govt. Hospital than did 2 month trainee course in Neonatology in 1992 from Kalawati Hospital Delhi. Since then he established his own setup which started from 4 beds & is increased to 75 beds exclusive pediatric hospital in last 2 decades. He has vast experience in field of pediatrics. He attended many national & international conferences.
