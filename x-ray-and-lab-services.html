X-Ray and Lab Services

Sethi Children Hospital offers outreach laboratory and imaging services (x-rays) for our patients with convenient Patient Testing Centres, to help prevent, find, or manage a medical problem.

Sethi Children Hospital’s Imaging Center and Lab Services is dedicated to providing a remarkable patient experience. Digital X ray, Out sourced (MRI, CT Scan, USG, 2D ECHO).

Biochemistry :- RFT, LFT, ABG, S.electrolytes, S.phosphors, S. uric acid, CPK – MB, CRP ( quantitative), Procalcitonin, Vit-D(T3, T4,TSH) levels, Growth Hormones Levels, Glycosylated Hemoglobin, CSF Analysis, Plerual fluid analysis, TTG.

Microbiology :- Bld C/S, CRP , Urine R/E . Urine C/S

Pathology :- CBC, ESR, Sepsis Screening, PBF, Retic Count, Malaria, Typhoid, Dengue, Scrub Typhus, Bone Marrow aspiration.

We Offer:
  • Fully automamted Biochemistry Analyzer
  • Five Part Hematology Analyzer
  • Digital X-Ray
  • Portable X-Ray
  • ECHO in PICU
  • Latest ABG Machine
