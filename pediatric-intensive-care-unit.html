Pediatric Intensive Care

Our PICU at SETHI CHILDREN HOSPITAL is directed by Paed. Intensivist, Chirag Sethi, who is qualified in managing critical cases & has done his fellowship in Paed. Intensive care from Sir Gangaram Hospital, New Delhi. Our unit at Sethi Children Hospital is 8 bedded & has round the clock availability of trained nursing staff & resident doctors on duty. PICU at Sethi children Hospital is state of art facility which is one of its kind in the city & one of the very few in state which is directed by Intensivist, certified from Indian Academy of Pediatrics —critical Care Branch.

    We Offer:
  • All Critical Care (Life Threatening and Dangerous Illness) for Children.
  • Ventilation, Non Invasive (BI PAP) Artificial Respiration Support.
  • Invasive Hemodynamic Monitoring, Central Line Insertion.
  • Peritoneal Dialysis.
  • NIBP, Multi Para Monitors, Central Monitoring.
  • Bed side ECHO.
  • Bed side EEG.
  • Bed Side X-Ray.
  • 24 hours laboratory support.

We have been most trusted health care innovators for 25 years. We glad for our rich history for health organizations.
