Our endeavour

Sethi Children Hospital

The idea of setting up such a hospital for child healthcare came in our director's mind nearly two decades ago, starting with 4 beds & is increased to 75 beds today. Paediatricians are not mere doctors – they are trusted partners in your child's health.

We are a unique centre of excellence for children's healthcare in the state, with a dedicated PICU and NICU managed by qualified intensivists 24/7.

Emergency Services at Night:

The ER is managed by two trained pediatricians 24x7 even on public holidays and weekends. In addition, at least one senior consultant stays within the hospital premises during nights, thus giving 24x7 senior consultant driven cover to handle any pediatric emergency.
