We are a unique centre of excellence for children's healthcare in the state

The idea of setting up such a hospital for child healthcare came in our director's mind nearly Two decades ago, when he started with 4 beds & is increased to 75 beds in last 2 decades. Paediatricians are not mere doctors – they are trusted partners in your child's health. At Sethi Children's Hospital, we put ourselves in the shoes of the parents and endeavor primarily for children's healthcare. Your child's good health, well-being and happiness are our priority.
